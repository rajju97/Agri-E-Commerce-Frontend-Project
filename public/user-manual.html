Platform Overview

What is GAIF and how does it work?

🌱
About GAIF

GAIF (Ganga Agri Innovation Foundation) is an agricultural e-commerce platform that connects farmers directly with consumers. The platform eliminates middlemen, ensuring fair prices for farmers and fresh, quality products for buyers.

⚙
How It Works
Register an Account
Create an account as a Customer (to buy) or Seller (to sell products).
Browse & Discover
Explore products across categories: Fertilizers, Seeds, Grains, Compost, and Tools.
Add to Cart & Order
Select products, choose quantities, and place your order with your preferred payment method.
Track & Receive
Track your order status and receive products at your doorstep.

Registration

Create your GAIF account in a few simple steps.

📝
How to Register
Navigate to the Registration Page
Click Register in the top navigation bar, or visit /register.
Fill in Your Details
Enter your email, mobile number (10 digits), and choose a password (min 6 characters).
Select Your Role
Choose Customer to buy products, or Seller to sell.
Confirm Password & Submit
Re-enter your password and click Register. You'll be redirected based on your role.
Registration Fields

EmailYesValid email format
Mobile No.YesExactly 10 digits
RoleYesCustomer or Seller
PasswordYesMinimum 6 characters
Confirm PasswordYesMust match password
💡
Tip: Already have an account? Click "Already have an account? Login" link at the bottom of the registration form.

Login

Access your account by signing in.

🔒
How to Login
Go to the Login Page
Click Login in the navigation bar, or visit /login.
Enter Your Credentials
Type your registered email and password.
You'll Be Redirected
Customers → Home Page • Sellers → Seller Dashboard • Admins → Admin Dashboard
⚠
Trouble Logging In? Check your email/password. Make sure Caps Lock is off. Contact admin for password reset.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I create an account? ▼
Click Register in the nav bar. Fill in email, mobile, role, password, and submit.
Can I be both a buyer and a seller? ▼
Yes! Sellers have all customer abilities. Register as Seller to both list and buy products.
What payment methods are available? ▼
Cash on Delivery (COD) — pay on delivery. UPI Payment — pay via UPI app. Credit/Debit Card — pay with bank card.
How do I track my order? ▼
Go to My Orders from the user menu (or /orders). Each order shows status: Pending, Confirmed, Shipped, Delivered, or Cancelled.
How do I use AI features as a seller? ▼
On Seller Dashboard, enter product name first, then click "AI Description" or "AI Image". Edit results before saving.
Is shipping free? ▼
Yes! All orders include free shipping.
How many images can I add to a product? ▼
Up to 5 images. Formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF. Max 5MB each. Auto-compressed.
Can I cancel an order? ▼
Buyers: contact seller/admin. Sellers: cancel from Manage Orders. Admins: cancel from Admin Dashboard.
